Elizade

Elizade is the brainchild of Chief Michael Ade-Ojo. The company evolved from a vision, apparently made manifest through divine favours; but which Chief Ade.Ojo readily accepted as a mission. The dream that gave birth to Elizade started with the relationship between Chief Ade-Ojo and his wife Mrs. Elizabeth Wuraola Ojo, when both met as students at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka in the early 60s. Chief Ade.Ojo, who was studying Business Administration as one of the pioneer students of that course then, had vowed to establish and run a business of his own within 10 years of his working life. (He fulfilled the vow within six years). The name, ELIZADE – coined from ‘Elizabeth’ his wife’s first name and ‘Ade” his own middle name instantly struck him as a viable company name.
